Why we need Quality Education?

Education is the key that will allow many other Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to be achieved. When people are able to get quality education they can break from the cycle of poverty.Education promotes gender equality and the decrease of inequality. It also makes it possible for everyone to live better, more sustainable lives.
Education is also crucial for developing individual understanding and building a more secure society.education is one of the most powerful and proven vehicles for sustainable development.

Defining Quality Education

A quality education touches on a number of topics that support overall development. The main goals are character development, social-emotional learning, and obtaining practical skills. High academic standards are also incorporated.
It is the promise of a high-quality education that students will acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to realize their full potential and lead fulfilling lives.
